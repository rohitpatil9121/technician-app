# Technician App — Kya Hai Aur Kaise Kaam Karega

*(Business owner ke liye saral overview)*

---

## Ek line me

Hamare technicians ke phone ke liye ek app, jisme unko apne din ke saare jobs
dikhenge aur wo har kaam — pahुंchne se lekar payment lene tak — app me hi
step-by-step complete karenge. App apne aap customer ko WhatsApp pe update bhejega
aur office ko sab kuch live dikhega.

Ye app hamari **existing website (manager dashboard + WhatsApp system) ke saath
judi hui** hai — alag se koi system nahi. Dashboard me jo job technician ko diya
jaata hai, wahi uske app me aa jaata hai.

---

## Technician ka ek din (example se samjhiye)

Maan lijiye **Ramesh** hamara technician hai:

1. Subah app kholta hai → uska naam, aaj ki date, aur uski **rating (⭐4.7)** dikhti
   hai. Upar teen number: **Pending**, **Aaj ke jobs**, **Aaj complete** kiye.
2. Niche uske saare jobs list me — purane bache hue, aaj ke, aur **repeat calls**
   (jinpe dobara paisa nahi lena).
3. Ek job khologa to customer ka naam, address, problem, aur purifier model
   dikhega — saath me **Call** aur **Maps** ke button.
4. **"Job Accept"** dabaata hai → customer ko apne aap WhatsApp pe message jaata
   hai ki technician aa raha hai.
5. **"Main nikal raha hoon"** dabaata hai → customer ko pahुंchne ka time (ETA)
   chala jaata hai.
6. Ghar pahुंch ke **"Pahुंch gaya"** dabaata hai → app uski **location (GPS)**
   record kar leta hai (proof ki technician sach me gaya).
7. Problem check kar ke form bharta hai: kya kharaabi hai, **paani ki TDS reading**,
   konse parts lagenge, aur **photo** (pehले/kharaabi/purana part).
8. **Estimate banata hai** — charge + parts ka total. Phir customer ko WhatsApp pe
   bhejta hai approval ke liye. (Customer na mile to manager approve kar sakta hai.)
9. Customer **haan** karta hai → kaam karne ke baad **machine ka output TDS** enter
   karta hai (proof ki paani ab saaf hai), aur **naye lagaye parts + nikaale gaye
   purane/used parts ki photo** leta hai (genuine parts aur sahi kaam ka proof).
10. Phir **payment** leta hai — **UPI / Cash / Card**, chahe to alag-alag milake.
    UPI me customer ko **QR** dikha sakta hai.
11. **"Job Close"** karta hai → "Job Closed Successfully" dikhta hai, agli service
    ka time note hota hai, aur Ramesh ko apni rating/incentive ka asar dikhta hai.

Pura kaam ek seedhi line me chalta hai — koi step miss nahi ho sakta.

## App me kya-kya hoga (screen-wise, simple)

**1. Login**
Technician apne mobile number + OTP se login karega. (Demo dikhane ke liye ek
"Demo Login" button bhi hai.)

**2. Home — Aaj ke jobs**
- Upar: naam, rating, **Online/Offline** toggle, notification ghanti.
- Jobs alag-alag hisson me: *purane pending*, *aaj ke*, *aaj complete*,
  *repeat calls (free)*, aur *escalations (urgent)*.
- Har job par: customer, area, time, problem, model, aur **visit charge** (₹250 /
  Free), aur Call/Maps/Open button.

**3. Job — pura kaam (8 step)**
Accept → Nikla → Pahुंcha → Problem check → Estimate → Approval → Payment → Close.
Har step pe customer ko **ek hi saaf WhatsApp message** jaata hai (faltu messages
nahi).

**4. Meri Reviews**
Technician ki average rating, kis cheez pe customers ne kitne stars diye (politeness,
pehli baar me fix, time pe pahुंchna, etc.), recent feedback, aur "rating badhao to
incentive badhega" wala nudge.

**5. Help / Support**
- Manager aur office ka direct call button, **Emergency** support.
- Common problems ka quick guide (Low Flow, Bad Taste, Leakage, No Water).
- **Customer ko kaise samjhaayein** — ready scripts (visit charge, TDS, approval
  maangna, gusse wale customer ko handle karna).

## Business ko kya fayda

✅ **Koi job kabhi nahi khoyega.** Har job app me track hota hai, shuru se band
hone tak.

✅ **Poora hisaab saaf.** Konsa charge, konse parts, kitne ka, kaise paisa aaya
(UPI/Cash/Card) — sab record.

✅ **GPS proof.** Technician sach me gaya ya nahi — location record hoti hai.

✅ **Galat charging nahi.** Charge type fix hai. **Warranty / AMC** wale ko free,
aur **10 din ke andar repeat** ko free — app khud sambhaal leta hai.

✅ **Bina approval paid kaam nahi.** Customer ki **manzoori ke बिना** paisa wala
repair nahi hoga — disputes kam.

✅ **Customer ko sahi time pe update.** Har milestone pe ek clean WhatsApp message
— na zyada, na kam.

✅ **Technician ki performance dikhti hai.** Ratings + repeat-complaint kam → seedha
incentive se juda.

✅ **Naye business ka mauka.** "Customer ko naya purifier ya special part chahiye?"
— ek tap me lead ban jaati hai, manager follow-up karega.

✅ **Sab kuch ek jagah.** Ye app, manager dashboard, aur WhatsApp — ek hi system,
ek hi data. Dashboard pe live dikhega technician ne kya kiya.
